Phil Swift is Back!

In Flex Tape Galaxy, the continuation of my Flex Tape Adventure series on scratch, Phil Swift embarks on an intergalactic adventure to save the Flex Tape Factory and Flex Seal Products. Phil encounters many galaxies of dank memes and glitchy platforming along the way!

I have no idea when this game will be finished and released. Depending on my motivation and the difficulty of level and boss design it could be anywhere from December to May. Keep up to date with my youtube for updates on the games development progress
